Contrada Cavalli

19 radically different homepage directions.

Each mockup is a standalone HTML file using the real club imagery already pulled into the repo. These are meant to be fast aesthetic comparisons, not final implementations.

Direction 01

Editorial Window

Quiet, airy, magazine-inspired. Looks like a modern sports venue with restraint.

Direction 02

Night Match

Dark, electric, sharp CTA bias. Feels like evening booking culture and fast energy.

Direction 03

Playful Club

Warm, friendly, bouncy. More social and local, less premium and less formal.

Direction 04

Premium Minimal

Calm and expensive-looking. Reduced copy, stronger whitespace, very direct actions.

Direction 02A

Scoreboard Neon

Still dark and electric, but more structured and sports-broadcast inspired.

Direction 02B

Poster Grid

A louder, more graphic version of the same night-booking identity.

Direction 04A

Gallery Minimal

Premium, spacious, image-led, with a softer resort-like tone.

Direction 04B

Atelier Club

Premium but more editorial and typographic, less architectural than 04.

Direction 02C

Local Club Dark

Dark sports look, but calmer copy and a structure much closer to the original website.

Direction 02D

Utility Vetrina

The simplest dark variant: service cards, contacts, and booking with almost no branding theatrics.

Direction 05

Refined Dark Club

Dark and calm, with quiet typography, olive accents, and a serious club-house feel.

Direction 06

Hospitality Light

Brighter, warmer, more venue-focused, like a well-designed place rather than a marketing page.

Direction 07

Ultra-Minimal Grid

Architectural, strict, and almost silent. Refined through spacing and proportion, not decoration.

Direction 08

Literal Vetrina

A calmer, more direct interpretation of the current website: only courts, contacts, and booking.

Direction 09

Newspaper Broadsheet

Italian broadsheet layout with multi-column grid, serif editorial type, and thin rules.

Direction 10

Mediterranean Warm

Terracotta and ochre palette, warm photography overlays, Italian courtyard feeling.

Direction 11

App-First Card UI

Native mobile app feel scaled to desktop. Tight cards, pill buttons, system-UI aesthetic.

Direction 12

Full-Bleed Cinematic

Full-viewport hero image, minimal text overlay, photography-forward like an architectural portfolio.

Direction 13

Italian Geometric

Vignelli-inspired. Strong grid, bold geometric shapes, red accent, confident whitespace.
